Welcome Message from the Headmaster

Welcome to Foothills Christian School. Here, we believe every child is a gift from God, created with unique potential to impact the world. Since our establishment in 1999, our Christ-centered community has been committed to nurturing students in faith, knowledge, and character.

We combine strong academic foundations with spiritual growth, guided by biblical principles and discipline in love. From Preschool to Senior High School, our students benefit from the Ghana Education Service (GES) curriculum as well as our newly accredited Oxford International Curriculum, preparing them to excel both locally and globally.
